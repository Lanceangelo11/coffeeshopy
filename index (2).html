Our Story

Discover the passion behind every cup we serve

Crafting Coffee Experiences Since 2015

Nestled atop one of the city's most charming buildings, Rooftop Café by Cee offers more than just coffee—we provide an escape from the ordinary. Our journey began with a simple love for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a space where people could connect, relax, and savor the moment.

Every bean we source is ethically grown and carefully roasted to highlight its unique character. Our baristas are trained artisans, passionate about extracting the perfect flavor from every cup. Whether you're seeking a quiet spot to work or a beautiful venue to meet friends, our rooftop oasis awaits.
